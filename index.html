About Me

Who Am I?

Hi I'm Liwen Kang I have a PhD in Architecture, Landscape Architecture and Urbanism from IIT, where I also worked as an Adjunct Professor for a year and a Teaching Assistant for four years. I have multiple publications and awards, including the Best Paper Award and the Best Defended Dissertation Award, for my innovative and interdisciplinary work on the urban ecology of Chicago. I have also gained practical experience as a Production Assistant at the Council on Tall Buildings and Urban Habitat, where I contributed to the design and editing of publications and reports on tall buildings and urban development. I am proficient in AutoCAD, Photoshop, InDesign, Illustrator, ArcGIS, SketchUp, and Rhino, and I hold a LEED Green Associate certification. I am passionate about advancing the knowledge and practice of landscape-based urbanism, and I aspire to collaborate with diverse and talented teams to create more livable, equitable, and beautiful cities. I speak Chinese and English fluently and I enjoy traveling and photography.
